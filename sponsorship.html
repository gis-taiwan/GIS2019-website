So far, First bank will proceed with digital transformation and take advantage of AI services as well as new technology to strengthen front desk services. Special branches will also be established to draw more customers.

Centurion

The “gulfstream” design on baggage cases is a renowned feature for CENTURION’s product. It is protected by the design patent in major countries around the world. The appearance resembles water pipes and is nicknamed “water pipe baggage case” on the Internet. In fact, the “gulfstream” design was inspired by letter “H” from the word “Harmony”, while endless streaming of “H” represents the unity among different ethnicities and races.
